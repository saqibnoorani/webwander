WebWander

Rediscover the internet.

Remember StumbleUpon? WebWander brings back the joy of discovering amazing websites — rebuilt for the modern web. Hit STUMBLE and go somewhere you never knew existed.

Add to Chrome — It's Free

How It Works

  1. Pick your interests — Choose from 16 topics during onboarding
  2. Set your adventurousness — Stick to what you love, or go full wild card
  3. Click STUMBLE or press Alt+S to discover a new site
  4. Like or dislike sites to teach WebWander your taste
  5. Save your favorites to collections for later

Features

🎯

One-Click Discovery

Hit STUMBLE and get whisked away to a hand-curated website matched to your interests.

🧠

Smart Recommendations

The more you rate, the better it gets. WebWander learns your taste over time.

📁

Collections

Organize your favorite discoveries into named collections for easy access later.

🏆

Badges & Streaks

Earn 14 badges as you explore — from First Steps to Pioneer. Keep your streak alive!

🎚️

Adventurousness Slider

Control how much we surprise you vs. staying on topic. Your call.

🔒

Privacy-First

No login, no email, no tracking. All data stored locally in your browser.

Keyboard Shortcuts

Alt+S Stumble to a new site (works anywhere in Chrome)
↑ Like the current site
↓ Dislike the current site
→ Stumble to the next site
S Save to a collection

The Floating Toolbar

When you stumble upon a site, a sleek toolbar appears at the bottom of the page. From there you can like, dislike, save to a collection, or stumble again — all without leaving the page you're on.

Open Source

WebWander is open source and free forever. Built with Svelte and Vite. Check out the code, report bugs, or contribute on GitHub.
